ABOUT VAHANAA

VAHANAA '18 is an Inter college/Institute National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Automobile Engineering, Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science, Chennai which is held every alternate year since 2008.

Auto folks all around Chennai gather here for the staggering Auto expo. It does not end there, various technical and non- technical events are also conducted which gives exposure, create a platform for students to display their skills and talent
